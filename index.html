About Me

I have recently graduated from the Lebanese American University - Byblos, Lebanon with a BSc in Computer Science. I am currently based in Beirut, Lebanon and originally from Tunis, Tunisia. I consider myself a fullstack developer. I enjoy building web applications especially when they follow the MVC architecture and work around Web APIs.

I enjoy playing around with cool open source technologies such as Node.js, Angular JS and R. When it comes to databases, NoSQL is the way to go for me. Thank you MongoDB!

I spend most of my spare time learning how to do new things and reading about the newest trends. When I am not doing that, I am probably traveling, taking a break from life, or chilling with my friends from the Art scene.

Latest Projects

Allemni Network

My final year project as a computer science student

New

This initiative aims at improving the educational experience by providing a content management solution for school administrators, educators and students and also direct support to high school students in the form of online tutoring. The platform proposed will start on a small scale by serving one high school community in Tunisia as a first step and eventually scale to serve the entire country and possibly the rest of the MENA region later.

The main objective of this project is to provide a web-based platform that facilitates the exchange of information between administrators, educators and students in an easy and practical way. The platform also provides high accessibility, flexibility and efficient collaboration between the involved parties.
